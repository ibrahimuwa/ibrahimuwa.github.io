# People

I have the privilege of supervising and co-supervising a talented group of HDR (PhD / MSc) candidates, visiting scholars and undergraduate / capstone students across **The University of Western Australia (UWA)** and **DPIRD**. Their work spans **3D vision and LiDAR**, **medical imaging**, **multimodal person re-identification**, **precision agriculture and drone analytics**, and **deep learning for remote sensing**.

A complete description of the projects each student is working on is available on the [Supervision &amp; Collaboration](/supervision/) page.
