# Supervision &amp; Collaboration

I supervise and co-supervise students at **UWA** across the **School of Physics, Mathematics &amp; Computing**, the **School of Optometry**, and **DPIRD-affiliated capstone projects**. My supervision is research-led: students contribute directly to ongoing collaborations with **Prof. Ajmal Mian (UWA)**, **Prof. Fred K. Chen and Dr. Khyber Alam (UWA Optometry / Lions Eye Institute)**, and **DPIRD scientists** working on precision agriculture and crop phenotyping. The full list of student names and contact details is on the [People](/people/) page.

## Current projects

### PhD — Generation and Geo-registration of 3D Point Clouds
*Department of Computer Science &amp; Software Engineering, UWA.*

This project develops large-scale generative and registration methods for **city-scale 3D point clouds**, combining **structured latent flow** for generation and **multi-sensor LiDAR / GNSS / IMU fine alignment** for accurate global registration. Outcomes feed directly into our 2026 *JSTARS* and *NeurIPS* / *ECCV* submissions.

- **Student:** Mr. Frank Wang (HDR Candidate) — [23631069@student.uwa.edu.au](mailto:23631069@student.uwa.edu.au)

### PhD — Classification of Inherited Retinal Diseases (FAF and Ultrawide Imaging)
*School of Allied Health (Optometry), UWA — co-supervised with Optometry / Lions Eye Institute.*

Deep learning for the **classification of inherited retinal diseases** from **fundus autofluorescence (FAF)** and **ultrawide retinal imaging**. The work targets clinically-deployable diagnostic support and contributes to a 2026 *Journal of Ophthalmology* publication.

- **Student:** Han Trinh — [han.trinh@uwa.edu.au](mailto:han.trinh@uwa.edu.au)

### PhD Visiting Student — GIR-CoT for Multimodal Person Re-Identification
*UWA visiting research project.*

**GIR-CoT: Geometry-grounded Identity Reasoning Chain-of-Thought** for **RGB–NIR–TIR–3D–Text Person Re-Identification.** The project introduces structured chain-of-thought reasoning grounded in 3D geometric cues for robust cross-modal person Re-ID under low-light, thermal and aerial conditions.

- **Student:** Xuefeng Tao — [xuefeng.tao@uwa.edu.au](mailto:xuefeng.tao@uwa.edu.au)

### MSc (CITS5014) — Early Detection and Subtype Classification of AMD
*Master of Information Technology, UWA.*

Deep learning models for the **early detection and subtype classification of Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD)** using multimodal retinal imaging.

- **Student:** Fiona Wei — [24610193@student.uwa.edu.au](mailto:24610193@student.uwa.edu.au)

### BSc Capstone — Automated Plant Counting (UWA × DPIRD)
*A collaborative project between UWA and the WA Department of Primary Industries and Regional Development (DPIRD).*

The team is building an **automated pipeline for plant counting per plot using drone imagery and object detection**, with direct application to APPN's national crop phenotyping infrastructure.

- **Students:** Andrew Mekhail, Joseph Harvey, Lychen Dai, Zhi Wang, Punit Patil — see the [People](/people/) page for contact details.
